\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents an office building, which is a type of commercial property. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classOffice}{Office}} class extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommercial}{Commercial}} class, providing specific functionalities related to offices, including management of entertainment and employment ratings, as well as maintenance operations.
