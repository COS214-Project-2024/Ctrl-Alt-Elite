\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a townhouse, inheriting from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classResidential}{Residential}} class. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTownhouse}{Townhouse}} class models a townhouse structure, allowing for operations related to occupancy rate calculations, maintenance, and management of residents.
