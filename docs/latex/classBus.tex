\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a bus for public transport. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classBus}{Bus}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classPublicTransport}{Public\+Transport}} and encapsulates the attributes and behaviors of a bus, including its fare and travel methods.
