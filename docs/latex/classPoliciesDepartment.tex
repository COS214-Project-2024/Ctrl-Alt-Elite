\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the policies department responsible for developing and enforcing public policies. 

Represents the policies management department of a city government.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPoliciesDepartment}{Policies\+Department}} class is responsible for managing city policies, implementing new policies, and handling taxation rates within the city.
