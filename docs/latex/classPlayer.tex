\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a player managing a city in the simulation. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPlayer}{Player}} class provides functionality to create and manage a city, including adding buildings, citizens, government, resources, and utilities. The player can also perform various actions related to city management and transport.
