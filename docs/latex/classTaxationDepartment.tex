\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the taxation department responsible for managing tax policies and revenue. 

Manages taxation and city management within a government framework.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classTaxationDepartment}{Taxation\+Department}} class is responsible for managing tax rates and handling notifications related to tax updates in a city. It inherits from the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGovernment}{Government}} class, allowing it to interact with the city\textquotesingle{}s governance system.
