\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a plane as a mode of public transport. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPlane}{Plane}} class extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPublicTransport}{Public\+Transport}} class, encapsulating the functionalities and properties associated with air travel, including ticket costs and the ability to travel.
