\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a commercial building, with attributes for entertainment and employment ratings. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classCommercial}{Commercial}} class inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilding}{Building}} and introduces specific features such as ratings for entertainment and employment, to reflect the role of commercial buildings in the city\textquotesingle{}s economy.
