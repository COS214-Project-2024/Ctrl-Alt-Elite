Represents a plant as a type of industrial facility. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classPlants}{Plants}} class extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classIndustrial}{Industrial}} class, encapsulating the properties and functionalities associated with industrial plants, including employment ratings and pollution levels.
