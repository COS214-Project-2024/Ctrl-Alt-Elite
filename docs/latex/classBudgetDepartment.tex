\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Handles budget allocation and financial management within the city government. 

Represents the budget department responsible for overseeing financial allocations and expenditures.

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classBudgetDepartment}{Budget\+Department}} is responsible for managing the city\textquotesingle{}s budget, including allocation and other financial adjustments. It extends the \mbox{\hyperlink{classGovernment}{Government}} class and overrides key methods related to budget management.
