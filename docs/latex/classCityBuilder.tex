\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the \mbox{\hyperlink{classBuilder}{Builder}} interface to construct complex city buildings for population, economic, and infrastructure expansion. 

\mbox{\hyperlink{classCityBuilder}{City\+Builder}} provides specific methods to support different aspects of city development, including population growth, job growth, housing expansion, economic development, and infrastructure expansion.
