Detailed Description

Represents the taxation department responsible for managing tax policies and revenue.

Manages taxation and city management within a government framework.

The TaxationDepartment class is responsible for managing tax rates and handling notifications related to tax updates in a city. It inherits from the Government class, allowing it to interact with the city's governance system.
