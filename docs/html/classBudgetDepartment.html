Detailed Description

Handles budget allocation and financial management within the city government.

Represents the budget department responsible for overseeing financial allocations and expenditures.

The BudgetDepartment is responsible for managing the city's budget, including allocation and other financial adjustments. It extends the Government class and overrides key methods related to budget management.
